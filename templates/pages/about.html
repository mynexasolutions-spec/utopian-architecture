About Us

Welcome to Utopia Furniture & Décor

Founded in 2021 by Architect Abdul Qadir, Utopia Furniture & Décor is a premier interior design and furniture customization firm based in Muzaffarnagar, UP. Backed by international and Gulf project experience, we blend global design standards with Indian sensibilities to create stunning, personalized spaces.

From luxury residences to modern commercial spaces across Delhi NCR and Uttarakhand, we deliver complete turnkey solutions from concept to execution—always prioritizing quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.
